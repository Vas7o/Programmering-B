ABOUT

Scarlxrd, born Marius Lucas Antonio Listhrop on June 19, 1994, is a British rapper known for his aggressive and unique style, blending elements of trap music with heavy metal and screaming vocals. His music often features dark, intense themes and a raw, unfiltered energy that has earned him a dedicated following, particularly among fans of alternative hip-hop and metal.

Early Life and Career Beginnings

Scarlxrd was born in Wolverhampton, England, and initially gained recognition as a YouTube personality under the name Mazzi Maz. Starting around 2012, he created comedic vlogs, challenges, and collaborations with other YouTubers, building a substantial online presence. However, after a few years, he grew disillusioned with this persona and sought a more genuine outlet for his creative energy.

Transition to Music

Around 2014, Marius shifted his focus from YouTube to music. He initially formed a nu-metal band called Myth City, where he was the lead vocalist. Myth City's music combined elements of rap, metal, and punk, which laid the groundwork for the aggressive sound Scarlxrd would later become known for. However, after releasing a few tracks and an EP, Myth City disbanded.

Birth of Scarlxrd

In 2016, Marius reinvented himself as Scarlxrd, a persona far removed from his YouTube days. He started releasing music that fused elements of trap, hip-hop, and metal, characterized by his intense, screamed vocals and dark, brooding beats. His breakthrough came with the 2017 single "Heart Attack," which became a viral hit and has since garnered millions of views on YouTube. Legacy and Impact

Scarlxrd's influence extends beyond music into fashion and internet culture. His rise from a YouTube comedian to a pioneering musician demonstrates his versatility and willingness to take risks. By embracing his darker, more authentic self, Scarlxrd has carved out a unique space in the music industry, inspiring others to follow their paths regardless of the conventions of the mainstream.
